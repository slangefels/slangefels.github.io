Noteworthy Games

According to Nintendo these games were the top selling titles for the 3DS as of March 31, 2025:

1. Mario Kart 7

18.99 million pcs.

Released in 2011

2. Pokemon X / Pokemon Y

16.78 million pcs.

Released in 2013

3. Pokemon Sun / Pokemon Moon

16.33 million pcs.

Released in 2016

4. Pokemon Omega Ruby / Pokemon Alpha Sapphire

14.67 million pcs.

Released in 2014

5. New Super Mario Bros. 2

13.42 million pcs.

Released in 2012

6. Animal Crossing: New Leaf

13.06 million pcs.

Released in 2012 (Japan), 2013 (Worldwide)

7. Super Mario 3D Land

12.89 million pcs.

Released in 2011

8. Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS

9.65 million pcs.

Released in 2014

9. Pokemon Ultra Sun / Pokemon Ultra Moon

9.26 million pcs.

Released in 2017

10. Tomodachi Life

6.72 million pcs.

Released in 2013 (Japan), 2014 (Worldwide)


As stated by Nintendo, the numbers provided reflect "...worldwide consolidated sales in units life-to-date."
